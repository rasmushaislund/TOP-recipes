Danish Tarteletter

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled and diced chicken breasts
  • 40 grams butter
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 4 deciliter milk
  • 200 gram tinned white asparagus
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 12 tartelet pastry cups
  • A handful of chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a pot and whisk in the flour a little by little
  2. Then whisk in the milk little by little until the sauce is evenly thick in consistency
  3. Bring the sauce to a boil and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes while stirring. Add salt and pepper for taste. Adjust consistency with asparagus water or chicken broth
  4. Add the diced chicken breast and the drained asparagus to the sauce
  5. Heat the tartelet pastry cups in the oven following the directions on the package
  6. Fill the warm tarteletter with the sauce and top of with chopped parsley and freshly ground pepper
  7. Eat right away. Enjoy
